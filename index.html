Disease of the Day Malaria
Fast Facts
Malaria — Plasmodium falciparum
Leading cause of fever in Nigeria. ~27% of global malaria deaths occur in West Africa. ACT is first-line. Use RDT before treating.
Quick Recall
AL = Artemether + Lumefantrine. Take with food (fatty meal boosts absorption 16×). Full 3-day course even if feeling better.

Pathophysiology & Etiology
Plasmodium spp. via Anopheles mosquito
Causative AgentP. falciparum (most lethal), P. vivax, P. malariae, P. ovale
MechanismParasite invades RBCs → hemolysis → cytokine release → fever cycles every 48–72h. Falciparum causes microvascular sequestration → cerebral malaria
TransmissionFemale Anopheles gambiae bite (night-biting), rarely blood transfusion or vertical (congenital)
